What will you get?

Our AI will generate a unique greeting based on the words you specify. You can also upload an image and the artificial intelligence will give it the touch it needs to be a spectacular greeting.

How does envIA work?

envIA is a creative generator based on the latest advances in artificial intelligence and natural language processing.

When you type text into our system, we feed it into a machine learning model that has been pre-trained with millions of texts to learn to write in natural language.

The model captures the meaning and context of your words, and is able to generate with them a grammatically correct, unedited, and? surprisingly coherent.

When you upload an image, we use an intelligent image-to-image model, which we communicate with with a simple instruction called a prompt.

In this case, we ask you to combine the image you have uploaded with a celebratory style, but respecting the people if they appear. The result is different every time and, above all, unpredictable.
